PetCarePal Chatbot Embedding Guide

This guide shows you how to embed the PetCarePal chatbot on your website. There are two main options:

Option 1: Direct Link

The simplest way to integrate the chatbot is to provide a link to the chatbot page:

<a href="./chatbot.html" target="_blank">Open Pet Health Assistant</a>

Try It: Open Chatbot in New Window

Option 2: Embed in an iframe

For a more integrated experience, you can embed the chatbot directly on your page using an iframe:

<iframe src="./chatbot.html" width="100%" height="600px" frameborder="0" title="PetCarePal Chatbot"> </iframe>
